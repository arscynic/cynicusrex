Non-toxic alternatives to corporate social media

  • Mastodon: “Social networking, back in your hands. Follow friends and discover new ones among more than 4.4M people. Publish anything you want: links, pictures, text, video. All on a platform that is community-owned and ad-free”.
  • Fediverse: “It is a common name for federated social networks running on free open software on a myriad of servers across the world.”
  • Matrix: Secure and independent communication, connected via Matrix.
  • Pixelfed, Fstoppers: share your photographs.
  • BookWyrm, Goodreads: share your books.
  • Last.fm, ListenBrainz: share your music.
